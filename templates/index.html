How can I complain a file ?

To file a complaint with a file attachment, compose a clear complaint letter describing the issue. Attach supporting documents and send it to the relevant recipient, such as a customer service department or regulatory agency, through email or physical mail. Ensure your contact information is included for their response. Follow up if necessary, keep records, and consider seeking legal advice if the issue remains unresolved.

How can I obtain a legal document?

To obtain legal documents, contact the relevant government agency, courthouse, or legal entity responsible for the document. Request it formally, providing necessary details and paying any required fees. Wait for processing and collect the document as instructed once it’s ready. Specific procedures may vary by location and document type.
