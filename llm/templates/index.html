Legal Topics

Family Law

Family law in India governs legal matters related to marriage, divorce, child custody, adoption, and inheritance, often influenced by religious and cultural diversity.

Property Law

Property law in India governs the rights and regulations related to the ownership, transfer, and use of real and personal property.

Labor Law

Labor law in India governs employment relationships, addressing workers' rights, wages, working conditions, and industrial disputes through various legislations and regulations.

Criminal Law

Criminal law in India encompasses offenses defined in the Indian Penal Code and other statutes, governing punishments for criminal acts.
